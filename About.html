About me

Pantelis Maniotis received his BSc and MSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTh), Greece, in 2015. He also finished his his PhD in Computer Science and Electronics Engineering from the University of Essex, in November 2020. He is a competent data scientist with a comprehensive knowledge and experience in analysing large datasets and developing forecasting models. Some of his interests involve the fields of machine learning, Deep-learning, time-series forecasting, and statistical analysis.
